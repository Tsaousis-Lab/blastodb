<h-hero>The Mission of BlastoDB</h-hero>

[:hero]


BlastocystisDB is dedicated to advancing global knowledge and collaboration on Blastocystis, one of the most prevalent yet underexplored microorganisms in the human and animal gut. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, accessible, and sustainable database that:

- **Integrates** genomic, transcriptomic, proteomic, and microbiome data on Blastocystis across hosts and environments.
- **Trustworthyness**. BlastoDB should be trustworthy. For this reason, we check the data propoted on this website and only feature reliable resources. Data here is linked to trustworthy publitations, and hostet on established services like Zenodo or the NIH.
- **Supports** the scientific community by offering reliable tools, protocols, and curated resources for accurate identification, subtype classification, and comparative analyses.
- **Fosters** collaboration across disciplines—spanning microbiology, parasitology, genomics, ecology, and clinical sciences—under the One Health framework.
- **Promotes** transparency, open science, and capacity building, ensuring researchers worldwide, including those in low- and middle-income countries, can contribute to and benefit from the resource.

By building this central hub, BlastocystisDB aims to accelerate discoveries, harmonize methodologies, and unlock the role of Blastocystis in health, disease, and microbiome dynamics.
