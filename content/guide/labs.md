<h-hero>Research Labs</h-hero>

Here you find the information on how to add, edit or remove research labs on the admin panel of BlastoDB ([www.blastodb.com/admin](/admin)). You also find an overview and description of the input fields.

## Add a new Research Lab

1. Click **Research Labs** in the left sidebar.
2. Click the single **Research Labs** file entry.
3. Scroll down to the bottom and click **+ Add Research Lab**. (This is easyer if you click *collapse all* on the top right).
4. Input the relevant Data.
5. Click **Save** on the top right. -> The Website will be updated automatically within a few minutes.


## Add a new Research Lab

1. Click **Research Labs** in the left sidebar.
2. Click the single **Research Labs** file entry.
3. Find the publication you want to edit. (This is easyer if you click *collapse all* on the top right).
4. Input the relevant Data.
5. Click **Save** on the top right. -> The Website will be updated automatically within a few minutes.

## Delete a Research Lab

1. Click **Research Labs** in the left sidebar
2. Click the single **Research Labs** file entry
3. Find the lab you need. (This is easyer if you click *collapse all* on the top right).
4. Click the "x" at the top right of the publication entery to delete it.
5. Click **Save** on the top right. -> The Website will be updated automatically within a few minutes.

It is save to remove research labs, as no other content links to them. Consider to delete uploaded images alongside the enteries to not clutter the repository data.

## Fields

1. **Lab Name** `required` - Name of the Lab
    - Enter via text input.
2. **Institution Logo** - Image or link to the image of the institutions logo
    - You can provide the institution logo either as an **uploaded file** or as a **URL** — at least one is recommended. If both are provided, the uploaded file takes priority.
      - **Uploaded logo:** SVG, PNG or WebP on a transparent background works best
      - **Logo URL:** A direct link to the logo image from the institution's own website
    - **Recommended size:** 1200 × 600 px (2:1 ratio)
    - **Format:** JPG or WebP
    - The image will be cropped to a fixed-height banner — use a landscape photo and avoid placing important content at the very top or bottom edges

3. **Short description** - Two to four sentences describing the lab's research focus
4. **Contact name** `required` - Name of the contact person
    - The full name of the primary contact person, with the title, e.g. `Dr Anastasios Tsaousis`.

5. **Contact email** `required` - The contact person's email address
6. **Contact webpage** - URL to the contact person's webpage
7. **Contact image** - Portrait of the contact person (optional)
    - **Recommended size:** 200 × 200 px minimum, square crop
    - **Format:** JPG or WebP
    - Ideally use a head-shot with the person not to far from the camera, otherwise they might not be visible.

8. **Name of Institution** `required` - Name of the university or company the lab is part of
9. **Address of Institution** `required` - Address of Institution or the Lab
    - Enter each line of the address on a separate line, e.g.:<br>School of Biosciences<br>Canterbury, Kent CT2 7NJ<br>United Kingdom
