<h-hero>Style Guide</h-hero>
[:hero]

BlastoDB lives at the intersection of rigorous science and open accessibility. The visual language is quiet and restrained — warm off-white backgrounds, deep teal accents that signal trust rather than excitement, and serif headlines that echo the authority of a printed journal. *Public Sans* keeps the interface crisp and neutral; *Crimson Pro* gives titles and hero text their weight. The result is a resource that feels serious without feeling cold: a place researchers can rely on and visitors can actually enjoy browsing.
