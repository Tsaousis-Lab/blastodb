**Components**

- Sterile horse serum                                        0,5 mL
- 1 % marmite solution[^1]                                   1,0 mL
- Buffer saline solution (pH 7,2)                        8,5 mL
- Rice starch                                                     30 mg

[^1]: Marmite is autolysed yeast extract made by Marmite Limited, London
