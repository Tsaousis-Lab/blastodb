# FAQ

**1. How do I order?**
You can order, or rather request items via the email specified for each entery. Keep in mind: The BlastoDB Biobank is not a webshop. Rather it is a platform where research labs can offer resources. Our job is to provide a platform where research labs show the availability of items, and connect you with people and research labs that can help you with your requests.

**2. What is the cost of each order?**
We do not provide concrete prices here, because the prices of each order can vary due to shipping, availability, and capacity at the offering research lab. Reach out to the contact people if you want to inquire about the cost of each item.

**3. How do I pay?**
BlastoDB does not handle any payments. Reach out to the labs offering resources, and their preferences about payment processing.
